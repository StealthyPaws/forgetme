CORE IDEA: Given a trained model w* and a point to remove (x,y), the Newton update
w⁻ = w* + H⁻¹·Δ approximates retraining from scratch. Loss perturbation b ~ N(0,σ²I)
added at training time certifies that the residual error cannot be distinguished by an adversary.
